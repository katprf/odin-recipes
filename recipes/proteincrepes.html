protein crepes

description

336 calories high protein crepes (whole plate with filling)

ingredients for the crepes

  • 33 gr plain flour
  • 16 gr protein powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 72 gr egg whites
  • 67 ml milk
  • 67 ml water
    •

    ingredients for the filling

    • 75 gr protein vanilla yogurt
    • 25 gr blueberries

    steps

    1. in a bowl mix the flour, protein powder and salt.
    2. add the egg whites, milk, and water to your dry mixture.
    3. whisk well to combine.
    4. bake in non stick pan.
    5. add your filling and enjoy.
