single serve protein cinnamon roll

description

single serve high protein low calorie cinnamon roll

ingredients

  • 30 gr plain flour
  • 20 gr vanilla protein powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 60 gr thick greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp sugar or sweetener
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

steps

  1. preheat your oven to 180 degrees celsius.
  2. combine the flour, protein powder and baking powder.
  3. add in your yogurt. mix it up then knead until it can form a ball. form a cylinder and roll it out.
  4. form a strip of around 20 cm x 10 cm, then slice it down the middle and join the two ends to create one long strip of about 40cm x 5cm.
  5. combine some sugar or sweetener with cinnamon and sprinkle it over the length of the dough. spread it out and lightly press it into the dough. roll the dough up into a scroll, place it into a greased ramekin and bake for 15-20 minutes.
  6. you can make your own icing by combing yogurt with a little icing sugar.
